## What is this?
This web app predicts the number of likes for short videos based on a few simple input features.

## Inputs
- **Number of Fans**
- **Days since upload**
- **Video length (seconds)**

## Output
- **Predicted Likes** (in the original scale)

## Notes
- The model was trained offline and loaded here for inference.
- Predictions are estimates and may be inaccurate for extreme cases.
